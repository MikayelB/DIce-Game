How to Play

1. Each player rolls the dice and gets numbers which add up in the "CURRENT" section
2. If the number on the dice is 1, then the player loses all his points in the "CURRENT" and the turn is passed to the next player.
3. In order to not lose the points, the player has to click on "HOLD" to get the points in the score section (and pass the tern to the next player).
4. The player who reaches 35 points first wins.
5. To restart the game from scratch, click on 'NEW GAME'.
